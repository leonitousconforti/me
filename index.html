Engineering Capstone Project

As a senior in my school's rigorous E3 engineering program, we spend the entire semester working on a capstone project of our choice with a partner. My partner and I both participate in robotics and love the game of soccer, thus we decided to create a robot that plays foosball against us. The game of foosball is based on the need for quick hand-eye coordination in order to move and rotate players to both block goals on defense and score goals on offense. Building a robotic system to both track the foosball on the playing surface, quickly move the player into position to block a shot, and then rotate the player to smack the foosball forward has certainly been challenging for us. Needless to say, we are very proud of the work we are accomplishing. Fingers crossed that the capstone exposition in the Spring will not be cancelled and we will be able to show off our ingenuity in person rather than virtually.
